The T-Beam Supreme is the go-to choice when you need GPS tracking or extended battery life. The swappable 18650 battery means you can carry spares for multi-day use.

## What's Included

- LilyGo T-Beam Supreme board
- Basic 915 MHz antenna
- 18650 battery holder (battery NOT included)

## Recommended Additions

- 18650 battery (Samsung 30Q or similar, 3000mAh+)
- Better antenna for improved range
- Protective case

## GPS Features

The built-in GPS allows:
- Location sharing with your group
- Breadcrumb tracking on hikes
- Asset tracking for vehicles or gear
- Waypoint marking

## Battery Life

With a quality 18650:
- 24-48 hours of active use
- Several days with power saving enabled
- Carry spare batteries for extended trips
